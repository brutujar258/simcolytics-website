About Simcolytics

Simcolytics is a specialized energy consulting firm located in Toronto, Canada with over 9 years of experience in battery energy storage systems (BESS) and photovoltaic (PV) design and implementation. Our team brings together advanced academic credentials including a Ph.D. in Petroleum Engineering with focus on computational physics and machine learning, combined with an MBA in Project & Strategic Management and professional certifications in both project management and engineering disciplines.

With a proven track record of managing high-risk engineering projects with budgets ranging from $500K to $2M USD, we deliver comprehensive renewable energy solutions enhanced by advanced machine learning and predictive analytics. Our expertise spans the entire project lifecycle, from initial feasibility studies and system design to permitting, installation, and commissioning. We specialize in battery storage solutions and our team members have successfully coordinated 150+ projects while serving as subject-matter experts for complex energy storage designs.

Our core strength lies in advanced data science and AI/ML capabilities, bringing cutting-edge machine learning solutions, predictive analytics, and intelligent energy management systems to energy optimization challenges. Our team holds cloud and AI certifications from leading technology providers, ensuring we stay at the forefront of emerging technologies in the energy sector.
